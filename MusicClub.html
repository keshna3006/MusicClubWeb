Who We Are

The Music Club at NITW is the oldest club in the history of the institute. The club houses the most talented artists including vocalists, drummers, guitarists, violonists, and floutists. The Music Club performs several times in the year but has two flagship events.

Events

The first concert is on the day 0 of Spring Spree and is called Open Air. The concert sees a footfall of nearly 2000 people every year, with the crowd growing exponentially every year. The second big event takes place during the Day-0 of the tech-fest.

Along with the two concerts, the Music Club also hosts the acoustic Lounge which sees a footfall of nearly 800 people. The club also performs at the Youth Fest, theme releases and official events of the institute. Other than events, the club hosts a 3-day workshop for students interested in vocals and instruments.

Three months before every event, artists form their bands, pick vocalists and instrumentalists, and choose a song. For the next two months, every band practices everyday in the club. Post two months, songs are screened and accordingly asked to be removed or worked upon. Before the event, there takes place a second screening to make sure everything is in place.

Our Team

Our team consists of two vital parts- the core team and the tech team. The core team consists of the chosen artists who are picked after several rounds of audition testing their skill, knowledge and presence. All vocalists and instrumentalists form the core team.

The tech team is responsible for other various duties of the club-namely, Stage design, social media presence, sound engineering, designing of posters, hosting and sponsorship work. The teams work together throughout the year to make sure every performance and every event is executed perfectly.
